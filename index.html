Vandana Shiva

Dr. Vandana Shiva is an Indian physicist, environmental activist, ecofeminist and anti-globalisation author. In 1991 she founded Navdanya , a national movement to protect the diversity and integrity of living resources, especially native seeds. She has published many books which challenge the dominant paradigm of non-sustainable, reductionist agricultural practices including:

  • Soil not Oil
  • Monocultures of the Mind: Perspectives on Biodiversity
  • Staying Alive
  • Biopiracy: the Plunder of Nature and Knowledge
  • Stolen Harvest: The Hijacking of the Global Food Supply

To find out more about Vandana Shiva's work visit Navdanya.org
